The Problem

A Market With No Right Tool

Artists have talent — but no dedicated stage to get booked
Independent musicians and performers rely on word-of-mouth. There is no structured way to attract event organizers.
Organizers waste hours manually searching for performers
Event planners scroll through social media and make calls to vet artists — slow, inconsistent, and unreliable.
General public cannot easily discover local talent
There is no single feed where the general public can explore local artists, follow them, and stay updated on events near them.

Discovery

The Video Feed

The general public opens the app to a full-screen, auto-playing video feed — similar to TikTok. Videos play as you scroll.

  • Filter by genre — Carnatic, Hip-Hop, Classical Dance, and more
  • Search artists by name, username, or genre keyword
  • Videos auto-tagged with genre and instruments by the artist
  • Contest-entry videos highlighted throughout the feed
  • Suspended accounts automatically filtered before the feed renders
How Artists Upload
Artists pick a video from their gallery. A thumbnail is auto-generated at the 1-second mark. Custom thumbnails also supported.
Safety Filter
Before the feed loads, the app fetches all suspended accounts and excludes their videos — users never encounter flagged content.

Communication

Real-Time Messaging

Any two users can chat in real time. Messages appear instantly via a live WebSocket connection — no polling, no refresh.

  • Message requests — must be accepted before a chat opens (prevents spam)
  • Typing indicator — shows when the other person is writing
  • Read receipts — see exactly when your message was seen
  • Online presence — shows if someone is currently active in the app
  • Unread badge counts update live on the conversation list
Powered by Supabase Realtime
The app subscribes to database changes on the messages table. A new message anywhere instantly pushes to all relevant open conversations.
ChatHub Screen
A central inbox lists all conversations sorted by latest message, with unread counts and last-message previews.

Artificial Intelligence

AI-Powered Insights

When an artist opens their dashboard, Google Gemini reads their metrics and generates plain-language advice — no jargon.

Metrics are gathered
Reach, engagement rate, follower growth, and booking conversion bundled into one object
Sent to Gemini securely
Via a Supabase Edge Function — the API key lives only on the server, never in the app
Insights displayed
2–3 short tips returned as JSON, each labelled positive / warning / suggestion

Example insight cards

Strong reach this week
Your profile was seen 40% more than last week — your recent video is driving new visitors.
Low engagement rate
Only 2% of viewers follow you. Try adding a call-to-action at the end of your videos.
Best time to post
Your audience is most active on Friday evenings. Schedule uploads between 7–9 PM.

Platform Management

The Admin Layer

A fourth hidden role — Admin — oversees the entire platform with tools invisible to regular users.

Content Moderation Queue
Review all reported videos and profiles. Approve or dismiss with one tap. See full resolution history with timestamps.
Event Approvals
Events created by organizers stay pending until an admin approves them for public listing on the platform.
Contest Management
Create and manage contests, review all submissions, select and notify winners in-app.
Platform KPIs
Total users by role, videos uploaded, active bookings, boost requests, and platform-wide engagement metrics.
