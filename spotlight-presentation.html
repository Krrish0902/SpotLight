The Problem

A Market With No Right Tool

🎸 Artists have talent — but no dedicated stage to get booked
Independent musicians, dancers, and performers rely on word-of-mouth. There is no structured way to attract event organizers.
📋 Organizers waste hours manually searching for performers
Event planners scroll through social media and make calls to vet artists — slow, inconsistent, and unreliable.
👥 Fans cannot easily discover local talent
There is no single feed where fans can explore local artists, follow them, and stay updated on upcoming events near them.

Discovery

The Video Feed

Fans open the app to a full-screen, scrolling video feed — similar to TikTok. Videos play automatically as you scroll.

  • Filter by genre — Carnatic, Hip-Hop, Classical Dance, and more
  • Search artists by name, username, or genre
  • Videos auto-tagged with genre and instruments by the artist
  • Contest-entry videos highlighted in the feed
  • Suspended accounts automatically filtered before the feed loads
How Artists Upload
Artists pick a video from their gallery. A thumbnail is auto-generated at the 1-second mark. Custom thumbnails are also supported.
expo-video Player
Uses the Expo video player with auto-play on scroll-into-view for a smooth, native-feeling experience on both iOS and Android.

Communication

Real-Time Messaging

Any two users can chat in real time. Messages appear instantly — powered by a live WebSocket connection, not polling.

  • Message requests — must be accepted before a chat opens (prevents spam)
  • Typing indicator — shows when the other person is writing
  • Read receipts — see exactly when your message was seen
  • Online presence — shows if someone is currently active in the app
  • Unread badge counts update live on the conversation list
Powered by Supabase Realtime
The app subscribes to database changes on the messages table. When a new message is inserted, all relevant open chats update immediately.
ChatHub Screen
A central inbox lists all conversations sorted by latest message, with unread counts and last-message previews.

Artificial Intelligence

AI-Powered Insights

When an artist opens their dashboard, Google Gemini reads their metrics and generates plain-language advice — no technical jargon.

1. Metrics are gathered
Reach, engagement rate, follower growth, and conversion bundled into one object
2. Sent to Gemini securely
Via a Supabase Edge Function — the API key lives only on the server, never in the app
3. Insights displayed
2–3 short tips, each labelled positive / warning / suggestion with distinct colours

Example insight cards

✅ Strong reach this week
Your profile was seen 40% more than last week — your recent video is driving new visitors.
⚠️ Low engagement rate
Only 2% of viewers follow you. Try adding a call-to-action at the end of your videos.
💡 Best time to post
Your audience is most active on Friday evenings. Schedule uploads between 7–9 PM.

Contests & Visibility Boosts

Contests

1. Admin creates contest
Sets a theme, submission deadline, and prize details
2. Artists submit videos
Entry is flagged and highlighted in the public feed for all users
3. Admin announces winner
Winner receives an in-app notification and badge on their profile

Profile Boosts

1. Artist requests boost
Chooses boost duration — e.g. 7 or 30 days
2. Admin approves
Profile is marked boosted and surfaces higher in organizer discovery results
3. Boost expires automatically
Countdown timer shown on profile; ranking returns to normal after expiry

Database Design

Key Data Tables

All data lives in PostgreSQL on Supabase. Row-Level Security ensures users can only read and write data they are authorised to access.

users
Role, email, account status (active / suspended)
profiles
Username, bio, genres[ ], city, DOB, avatar URL
videos
Title, URL, view / like counts, contest entry flag
messages
Chat messages with sender, read status, timestamp
gig_bookings
In-chat gig offers with event details and accept / decline status
bookings
Formal booking requests from organizer to artist
events
Event pages with lineup JSON and admin approval status
analytics_events
Every user action with geo, session, and demographic data
